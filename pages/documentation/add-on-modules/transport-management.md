# Transport Management 

1.	Click Transport on the left pane    

![Screenshot](./images/transport/a1.png)

2.	Click stoppage on the side menu

3.	Click Add button on the right top corner 

![Screenshot](./images/transport/a2.png)

4.	Record all the details in the form and click submit

![Screenshot](./images/transport/a3.png)

<span style="color:red">Note:Active/ Inactive options are available</span>

5.	The stopping list will be listed as shown below

![Screenshot](./images/transport/a4.png)

<span style="color:red">Note : Edit & Delete options are available in the actions tab</span>


## Route Management 

![Screenshot](./images/transport/a5.png)

2.	Click Route from the side menu

3.	Click Add button on the right top corner


![Screenshot](./images/transport/a6.png)

4.	Record all the details and click submit
